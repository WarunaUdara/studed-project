## Design Invariants (all screens)

1. Parchment canvas `oklch(0.984 0.003 95)`, Ink text `oklch(0.21 0.034 264.7)`.
2. One accent family per region — blue for action, green for growth, violet for AI, amber for achievement.
3. 24px card radius, pill buttons, borders over shadows.
4. Serif for thought, sans for function.
5. Motion: 150–450ms, spring-gentle, reduced-motion safe.
6. Mobile-first, 44px touch targets, bottom sheets over modals.
